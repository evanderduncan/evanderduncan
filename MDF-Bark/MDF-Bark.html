MDF_Bark

An image of tree bark laser cut into MDF board, 2022.

Made during the elective module - Art, Science, and Visual Thinking

Imitating nature through technology. Both the pattern and the product
it's engraved into replicate the source material.

When I have access to a laser cutter again I will refine this processes
and create a series of cabinet doors, each door engraved with a different
species of tree, featuring a different pattern of bark.
