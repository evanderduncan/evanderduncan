CAPTCHR
Honours Project patterns MDF_Bark Circulation Lichen batch1 batch2 batch3
A series of patterns inspired by the tile patterns found in Glasgow
tenement closes and the
structural forms of exposed piping.
Using node based TouchDesigner, and following a series of YouTube tutorials,
I combined a series of modified noise, CHOP, and shape values. Each batch of renders
showcases experimentation of values, resulting in outputs that range from industrial
structure to more organic form.
